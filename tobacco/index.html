Delaware Proposes Higher Tobacco Taxes, Risking Regressivity and Revenue Volatility

Wednesday, 03/11/2026
Delaware is proposing higher taxes on tobacco products like cigarettes and vaping products, which will make these items more expensive for consumers. The exact price increase isn't specified, but the state aims to collect an additional $18.9 million annually from these higher taxes.
Products: cigarettes, cigars, vaping products, and other tobacco items
